Drop-in Help Lab

Optional homework help lab - you can also volunteer: Drop-in Lab starts usually the second week of the semester. It is usually in STC 375 on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays from 3:30 – 6:30. These labs are not required, but are encouraged if you are struggling with any concepts, or as a place to work on assignments.
