Technology and Ethics

W01 - Reflect on Technology and Ethics. Interview 5 people.

Take note of trends and valuable insights while asking 5 people these questions below (you will submit trends and insights):

  1. Do you think social media apps are healthy? Why or why not?
  2. How might an app be unethical but profitable?
  3. How might ethics and living the gospel of Jesus Christ help us with the development of technology?
  4. How can living the gospel of Jesus Christ help us manage personal technology?
