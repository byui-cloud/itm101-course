Technology and Ethics

W01 - Reflect on Technology and Ethics Interview 5 people.

Ask 5 people and take note of trends and valuable insights:

  • 1- Do you think social media apps are healthy? Why or why not?

  • 2- When might an app not be ethical but profitable?

  • 3- How can having ethics and focusing on Jesus Christ help us with the development of technology?

  • 4- How can Jesus Christ help us manage personal technology?
