ITM 101 - Interview 10 People

Interview 10 People

  1. Ask each person the following questions:
    1. How much time do you spend using computing technology (computer, iPad, smartphone, smart TV, Kindle, etc.) every day?
    2. What is your favorite social media app?
    3. If you have a smartphone, what is the most used app on your smartphone?
    4. What websites or smartphone apps do you use to shop (buy things)?
  2. While interviewing a person, enter the person’s responses to the questions in this Google form so that everyone’s responses will be recorded.
