Why Meet Other People

Being a skilled collaborator and effective communicator are desired outcomes of BYU-Idaho. Reaching out to someone new is a great way to obtain these skills and outcomes. Meeting people in your industry can significantly improve your options for future job opportunities, which can also increase earning potential.

Many students have found a faculty mentor to be a friend and someone they can reach out to about questions related to their choice of major, finding internships, and many other invaluable tips to prepare them for their future.

Someday you may need to reach out to a new co-worker or supervisor and this skill set can prepare you for that. Having a skill set to meet new people may even help you find a friend or your future spouse.

  2. Contact your faculty mentor and make an appointment to meet with them next week.
