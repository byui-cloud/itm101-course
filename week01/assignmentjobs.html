ITM 101 Week 1 - Jobs Assignment

Find jobs you might want someday

Use instructor provided ChatGPT/AI prompt to find career paths. Paste this into your favorite AI generator:

"You are a tutor. Act as an adviser to me and what for my response on each questions. Find a career path for me. Help me find what jobs I might like. Ask me some questions to see what career might be best for me."

Answer the questions to find some jobs you might enjoy. If you already have a list of jobs you want, you can skip the first step.

Find and provide links to at least 5 future jobs or internships (5 points) that you are interested in on indeed.com or linkedin.com. Create that list of at least 5 jobs you want when you graduate or a list of internships that you find online.

Make a list of projects that you want to work on from the job requirements (5 points) or preferred requirements that you found in the job listings. For example, if one of the jobs prefers candidates that have used AWS Lambda functions, then make a list of the those skills like "AWS Lambda Functions."

Find a class at BYU-Idaho that teaches that and if you can't find one, ask around. If you still can't find one, spend a weekend doing it or try to incorporate it into class assignment you already have if possible.
