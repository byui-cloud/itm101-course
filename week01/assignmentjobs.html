ITM 101 Week 1 - Jobs Assignment

Find jobs you might want someday

Use the following prompt with ChatGPT or another generative AI to help you find paths. Paste this prompt into your favorite AI generator:

Act as a my adviser to help me find a career path. Ask me some questions and wait for my response on each question. Then suggest some careers that might be best for me.

Answer the questions to find some jobs you might enjoy. If you already have a list of jobs you want, you can skip the previous step.

Find and provide links to at least 5 future jobs or internships (5 points) that you are interested on indeed.com or linkedin.com. Create that list of at least 5 jobs you want when you graduate or a list of internships that you find online. I would recommend you find 3 internships and 2 full-time jobs.

Make a list of projects that you want to work on from the job requirements (5 points) or preferred requirements that you found in the job listings. For example, if one of the jobs prefers candidates that have used AWS Lambda functions, then make a list of the those skills like “AWS Lambda Functions”.

Find a class at BYU-Idaho that teaches that and if you can’t find one, ask around. If you still can’t find one, spend a weekend doing it or try to incorporate it into class assignment you already have if possible.

Submit links to 5 jobs and 5 skills you want to develop.
