ITM 101 In-class Activity Day 2 - Part 1

Overview

Brainstorm something cool to build as a team using AI

Deliverable: Choose one project to build as a team each week. Make sure it solves a problem/pain/issue and is viable to make money. Define six features or functionalities of your project. Turn in a screenshot of and describe your personal contribution.

Today's Submission

    Team Submission:

  • Only one person has to turn in the team content.
  • A 1-2 paragraph description of your cloud software/solution.
  • What problem/pain/issue does it solve?
  • Is it viable and how will you make money?
  • A bullet-point list of at least 6 features or functionalities.
  • Include a list of everyone's first and last names in a comment.

    • Individual Submission:

  • Join your team/table group on the "People" tab.
  • Each person needs to submit an artifact and description of their personal contribution to the team from today.
  • What ideas did you come up with?
