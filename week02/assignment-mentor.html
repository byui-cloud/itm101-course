ITM 101 Week 2 - Meet Faculty Mentor Assignment

Why Meet Other People

Being a skilled collaborator and effective communicator are desired outcomes of BYU-Idaho. Reaching out to someone new is a great way to obtain these skills and outcomes. Meeting people in your industry can significantly improve your options for future job opporuntities, which can also increase earning potential.

Many students have found a faculty mentor to be a friend and someone they can reach out to about questions related to their choice of major, finding internships, and many other invaluable tips to prepare them for their future.

Someday you may need to reach out to a new co-worker or supervisor and this skillset can prepare you for that. Having a skillset to meet new people may even help you find a friend or your future spouse.

Meet Your Faculty Mentor

Find and meet your faculty mentor and take a picture with them. Also, describe one tip or advice they gave you. You can upload the picture and put a comment in the submission of the tip or advice they gave you.
