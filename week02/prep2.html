Week 2 Day 2 Preparation

You have an upcoming assignment to attend a BYU-Idaho society event. You will need to take a picture of yourself at the event and write about something you find interesting or learned. For today’s preparation, you must choose a society and learn when and where they meet and plan to attend this week or next week.

Choose a Society to Attend

The Computing Society is the primary society for the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). They meet almost every Wednesday at 5:00 PM in STC 394, and you are welcome to attend.

Or you can attend one of the other CSE Department’s societies.

Or you can attend one of BYU-Idaho’s academic societies.

Attend the Society

Attend the society that you chose this week or next week so that you can submit the attendance assignment at the end of next week. When you attend, remember to take a picture of yourself at the event and write something you found interesting or learned.
