ITM 101 Week 5

Complete an Informational Interview

You can do this assignment as a team.

Learn more about informational interviews:

  • https://www.byui.edu/career/00000184-6210-d173-a3fe-fa34a7490000
  • https://www.byui.edu/career/students/know-your-audience
  • https://www.byui.edu/career/students/job-market-readiness

Find a job or an internship that you are interested in (or look at the list you already made) from indeed.com or linkedin.com or from a list of companies. Find a BYU-Idaho or BYU alumni working in those fields or at those companies and reach out to them to find out more about their job and how they got there.

Meet with and learn from them. Write a description of what advice you liked the most and the name of the person you met with.

You can do this as a team if you would like.
