Project Management & Planning

Your team this week will work on Project Management and Planning!

  • Why and How: Watch this video Create a Detailed Project Timeline with Milestones Using Generative AI
  • Task: Create a simple timeline for the project. Break the project into phases and give an estimated timeline for each.
  • Business Deliverable: Upload A short timeline showing phases and durations in Microsoft Word or Excel.

Example Timeline (Don't use this but use the Development Timeline instead):

    1. Planning & UX Design: 2 weeks
    2. Development: 8 weeks
    3. Testing & Debugging: 4 weeks
    4. Launch & Marketing: 2 weeks

Example Development Timeline (8 weeks):

    1. Feature 1 - Your first feature...: 1 week
    2. Feature 2 - Your second feature...: 3 weeks
    3. Feature 3 - Your third: 3 weeks
    4. Feature 4 - Your fourth: 1 week
