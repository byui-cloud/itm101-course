ITM 101 Week 6 Day 2 In-class Activity

Risk Assessment

As a team, identify potential risks to your project and how you would address them. This is an important part of product management.

  • Why and How: Watch this video Project Risk Identification Methods
  • Task: Identify at least two potential risks to the project and how you would address them.
  • Business Deliverable: A short paragraph explaining the risks and possible solutions.

Example (You can't use these for your team submission):

  • Risk 1: The app may not attract enough users. Solution: Create a marketing plan targeting students and professionals through social media.
  • Risk 2: Delays in development due to technical challenges. Solution: Schedule buffer time and hire an additional developer if needed.

Improve Security or Authentication as a team.

  • Tech Deliverable: Build something new or improve a previous project in this class as a team implementing/improving security or authentication. Use ChatGPT - Be creative. Have fun. If you fail that is ok, turn in what you did. Turn in a screenshot, URL, or description of what you did/improved/deployed in AWS as a team. Describe your personal contribution.
