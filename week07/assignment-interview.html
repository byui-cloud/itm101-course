Week 7 - Complete an Informational Interview

Find a job or an internship that you are interested in (or look at the list you already made) from indeed.com or linkedin.com or from a list of companies. Find a BYU-Idaho, BYU alumni, or someone you have a connection to working in your field of study or at those companies and reach out to them to find out more about their job and how they got there.
