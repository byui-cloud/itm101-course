Week 8 Day 2 Preparation

Resumé Template

Find a resumé template and start writing your resumé. You can choose a resumé template from the BYU-Idaho Career Center list of resumé templates or from some other place that you find.

How to Submit

Upload the template you chose or your actual resumé if you have one.

Future Assignment

Make sure you continue to build and write your resumé because you will submit it in a future assignment. Use the BYU-Idaho Career Center resumé resources to help you write your resumé.
