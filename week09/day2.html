ITM 101 In-class Activity Week 9 Day 2

Backup, Upgrade, and Restore

Some of the benefits of VMs or virtualized machines/servers in the cloud are the abilities to make backups (snapshots), to add RAM or processing power, and to restore from backups.

Create a snapshot, add more RAM to your server, modify something else like a file on the server, and restore your server from the backup (snapshot). Help your team. Take screenshots or make a video to turn in.
