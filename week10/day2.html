Project Management

Use Project Management Methodologies Today - Scrum agile stand-up meeting

Teams often use project management methodologies to ensure the project progresses. Daily stand-up meetings is one way to accomplish it.

Practice a daily stand up half way through the assignment today.

  1. First, each team member set a 'sprint' goal of what you will accomplish for the first half of class.
  2. Half way through the teamwork time, stop and answer these questions:
    1. What did you work on yesterday (assume that 'yesterday' is the first half of class)?
    2. What are you going to work on today (assume that 'today' is the last half of class)?
    3. What issues are blocking you (what are the blockers)?
