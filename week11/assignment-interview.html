ITM 101 Week 11 - Practice Interviewing

Objective

The objective of this assignment is to introduce students to VMock Interviews and learn and improve interviewing skills based on reviewing feedback from VMock.

Watch the following videos:

https://youtu.be/L979IKi2p5U

https://youtu.be/GmP7nl4h5Kk

https://youtu.be/5WZVf8Dsh_U

Complete the following steps:

Step 1: Log into Vmock using your university ID and password. (Please note, do not access VMock through Google. It will require you to pay to use it. Please use the portal found on the Career Center website to access your free account.)

Step 2: On the left side of the student dashboard scroll down and click on "Interviews"

Step 3: Next select "Mock Interview" and then select " Career Preferences". Add a preference according to your field of study.

Step 4: Complete a practice mock interview.

Step 5: Review the feedback, implement it, and complete a second mock interview.

What you will turn in:

Submit an image of the feedback received from your first and second practice interviews.
