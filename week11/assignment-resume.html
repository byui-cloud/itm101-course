ITM 101 Week 11 - Submit Your Resume to VMock

Submit your resume to VMock to get instant free AI feedback. The university provides this to students with up to 10 uploads per calendar year. Upload your resume or revised resume.

Objective:  

The objective of this assignment is to introduce students to the VMock résumé module, learn how to upload a résumé, receive a score and review all the feedback received, update and make changes based on the feedback and improve their résumé.

Watch the following videos:

Complete the following steps:

Step 1: Upload your résumé to VMock.

Step 2: Review all feedback VMock provided.

Step 3: Update your résumé using the feedback from VMock and the videos.

Step 4: Upload your résumé again and watch your score improve. (Please note: you only have 10 résumé uploads per year, January - December. If you are not able to improve your score after the 2nd upload in VMock, please schedule an appointment with a Career Success Mentor in Handshake for help so you do not waste your uploads.)

 

What you will turn in:

Submit your before and after résumé and scores from VMock.
