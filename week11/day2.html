ITM 101 In-class Activity Week 11 Day 2

Deliverable: As a team, create a list of things you should to prepare for a job interview. Use the Internet or ChatGPT - Be creative. Have fun. Turn in a screenshot, URL, or description of what you did as a team. Describe your personal contribution.

Practice a mock interview with a buddy, get feedback, and practice again. Switch turns.

Repeat with another buddy - SPEED DATING/INTERVIEWING STYLE.
