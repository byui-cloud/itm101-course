ITM 101 In-class Activity Week 11 Day 2

Make a marketing plan for you to prepare for interviews. Make a team marketing plan for your app. Practice Interviewing.

Marketing

Why and How: Refer to this video: Creating a Marketing Plan (11 min)

Task: Create a marketing plan for you to prepare for interviews and for your team to market your app.

Marketing You - Business Deliverable: As a team, create a list of things you should do prepare for a job interview.

Team - Business Deliverable: Make a marketing plan for your app you built this semester. It should include short-term and long-term marketing goals, a description of your target audience and their persona, one or more high level marketing strategies, a customer journey flow, analytics, and a click funnel process.

Team - Tech Deliverable: Build some artifacts of the plan. PDF of plan. Implement Analytics. Diagram of Customer Journey. Diagram of Click Funnel. Build a sales/squeeze page. Divide these up among your team to build.

Interviewing

Individual Deliverable: Have 5+ good stories with quantifiable results from your past experiences you can share to answer the Top 60+ interview questions.

Use the STAR method to prepare your answers: Situation, Task, Action, Results.

Mock Interview: Practice a mock interview with a buddy, get feedback, and practice again. Switch turns.

Practice Again: Repeat with another buddy - SPEED DATING/INTERVIEWING STYLE.
